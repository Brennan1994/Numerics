Detailed Description

The Adaptive Movement (Adam) optimization algorithm. The objective function must be differentiable and convex.

Authors: Haden Smith, USACE Risk Management Center, cole..nosp@m.h.sm.nosp@m.ith@u.nosp@m.sace.nosp@m..army.nosp@m..mil

Description: Adaptive Movement Estimation algorithm, or Adam for short, is an extension to the gradient descent optimization algorithm. Adam is designed to accelerate the optimization process, e.g. decrease the number of function evaluations required to reach the optima, or to improve the capability of the optimization algorithm, e.g. result in a better final result.

References:

  • Kingma and Ba (2014) Adam: A method for Stochastic Optimization
