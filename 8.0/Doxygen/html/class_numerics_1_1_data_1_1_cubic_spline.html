Detailed Description

A class for cubic spline interpolation.

Authors: Haden Smith, USACE Risk Management Center, cole..nosp@m.h.sm.nosp@m.ith@u.nosp@m.sace.nosp@m..army.nosp@m..mil

Description: Spline interpolation uses a piecewise polynomial called a spline as the interpolant. Instead of fitting a single, high degree polynomial to all of the given values at once, spline interpolation fits low-degree polynomials to small subsets of values. For cubic spline interpolation, polynomials of degree 3 are used.

References:

  • "Numerical Recipes, Routines and Examples in Basic", J.C. Sprott, Cambridge University Press, 1991.
